# **Privacy Policy for “One Thing”**

**Last Updated:** March 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the **“One Thing”** mobile application (the “App”).

“One Thing” is developed and operated by an independent developer. In this policy, “I”, “me”, and “my” refer to the developer of the App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

# **1\. Information I Collect**

I collect only the information necessary to provide and improve the functionality of the App.

## **1.1 Information You Provide**

When using the App, you may voluntarily provide information including:

### **Account Information**

If you choose to create an account, the App may collect:

* Name

* Email address

* Password (securely managed through the authentication provider)

Account creation is optional. Some features of the App may be accessible without creating an account.

### **User Content**

You may choose to enter content into the App such as:

* Your daily “One Thing”

* Reflections or journal entries

* Completion notes

* Optional status messages

* Optional profile image

This content is stored to support the functionality of the App.

### **Photos**

If you choose to add a photo to document completion of your “One Thing”, the App may request permission to access:

* Your device camera

* Your photo library

Photos are used only for your personal progress tracking within the App.

---

# **2\. Automatically Collected Information**

When you use the App, certain technical information may be collected automatically, such as:

* Device type

* Operating system version

* App version

* Crash reports

* Diagnostic information

This information helps improve app performance, reliability, and stability.

This data does not directly identify you.

---

# **3\. How Your Information Is Used**

Information collected through the App may be used to:

* Provide and maintain the App

* Save your daily “One Thing”

* Track streak progress and completion history

* Store reflections and optional completion photos

* Generate suggestions and prompts

* Send notifications related to your daily goal

* Improve the functionality and user experience of the App

* Provide user support

Some features may use automated systems or AI-powered services to generate suggestions or analyze reflections to provide helpful prompts.

User-generated content stored in the App is **private by default** and is not publicly visible unless a future feature explicitly allows sharing.

---

# **4\. Notifications**

The App may send notifications such as:

* Reminders to enter your daily “One Thing”

* Midday reminders about your goal

* Completion encouragement

You can disable notifications at any time through your device’s system settings.

---

# **5\. Photos and Camera Access**

If you choose to add a photo when completing your “One Thing”, the App may request permission to access:

* Your device camera

* Your photo library

Photos are stored only for your personal records within the App and are not publicly shared.

You can revoke camera or photo permissions at any time through your device settings.

---

# **6\. Data Storage and Security**

The App uses secure third-party services, including **Firebase**, to store and manage application data.

Reasonable measures are taken to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

---

# **7\. Subscriptions and In-App Purchases**

“One Thing” offers optional paid features that enhance the experience of the App.

### **Subscriptions**

Users may choose to subscribe to unlock additional features such as:

* Unlimited AI-powered suggestions

* Streak repair tools

* Calendar history viewing

* Additional customization options

* Additional streak management features

Available subscription periods may include:

* Weekly

* Monthly

* Yearly

Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled through your **Apple App Store or Google Play Store account settings**.

You can manage or cancel subscriptions at any time through your device’s app store settings.

---

### **Consumable In-App Purchases**

The App may also offer optional one-time purchases, including items such as:

* Additional streak freezes

* Suggestion refresh packs

These purchases are optional and enhance certain features within the App.

Consumable purchases are processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store and may not be refundable except as required by applicable law or store policies.

---

### **Payment Processing**

All payments are securely processed through the **Apple App Store or Google Play Store**.

I do **not collect or store payment card information**. Payment information is handled entirely by the respective app store platform according to their privacy policies.

---

### **AI-Powered Features**

Some features may use automated systems or AI-based services to generate suggestions or provide reflection prompts.

These services may process user input such as:

* Previous “One Thing” entries

* Reflections

* Goal history

This processing occurs only to provide functionality within the App and is not used for advertising purposes.

---

# **8\. Sharing of Information**

I do **not sell or rent your personal information**.

Information may be shared only in limited situations, including:

* With trusted service providers required to operate the App (such as cloud hosting or authentication services)

* If required by law or legal process

* To protect the safety, rights, or integrity of users or the App

If social features are introduced in the future, you will have control over what information is visible to other users.

---

# **9\. Data Retention**

Information is retained only as long as necessary to provide the App and its features.

If you delete your account, personal data and stored content may be deleted within a reasonable timeframe unless retention is required for legal or operational reasons.

---

# **10\. Children’s Privacy**

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13\.

I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13\. If it is discovered that such information has been collected, steps will be taken to delete it.
